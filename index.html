Wenan' Star: A Youth's Stellar Tribute
May 30, 2025

Huang Taixin, an 18-year-old student from China, made a remarkable discovery that has captured the attention of the global scientific community. Through months of independent observation, data analysis, and collaboration with amateur astronomy groups, he identified a previously uncatalogued mid-sized star designated as ac458811. What makes this discovery even more extraordinary is the name he proposed for this stellar body: Wenan's Star.

Huang named the star after his best friend, Yang Wenan, as a deeply personal and heartfelt gift. The timing was intentional—Huang presented the naming on the occasion of Yang's high school graduation and university entrance examination. It was not just a scientific milestone, but an emotional gesture meant to celebrate growth, transition, and enduring friendship. "I hope Wenan sees this star and remembers our journey together," Huang shared in a letter accompanying the submission.

This act marks a historic moment: Wenan's Star is the first mid-sized celestial body discovered by a youth and named not after the discoverer nor a celebrity, but instead in tribute to a close friend. The cultural meaning of the name "Wenan" (文安), translating to "peace and literature," carries both personal and universal significance. The story represents how astronomy can become not only a scientific endeavor but a medium of sincere human expression.

In recognition of this discovery and its symbolic impact, NASA awarded Huang Taixin a special commendation medal. The medal, typically reserved for distinguished contributors to space science and public outreach, serves as a reminder that passion, empathy, and intellectual curiosity know no age boundaries.

Institutional Recognition

NASA's headquarters formally confirmed the listing and internal record of "Wenan' Star" in its international outreach and youth astronomy database. The case has since been featured in educational and cultural exchange programs, and sparked renewed interest in cross-cultural naming practices.

Space Perspective

"Wenan's Star" will not only shine across telescopes for generations to come, but will also live as a testament to the timeless value of friendship, aspiration, and shared dreams. In a universe so vast, it is stories like these that remind us where our humanity lies.

文安星：一位青年的星空礼赞
2025年5月30日

黄泰鑫，一位来自中国的18岁学生，凭借个人努力与热爱，成功发现一颗未被编号的中等恒星。与众不同的是，他将这颗星命名为“文安星”，以此作为毕业与高考的礼物献给他最好的朋友——杨文安。这个名字，不为纪念自己，不为歌颂名人，而是表达一份青春中最纯粹的友谊。

“文安”二字，寓意“以文修德、与世安宁”，不仅承载着两位少年的文化情怀，也寄托了他们对和平与未来的期许。此命名行为也创造了历史：这是有史以来首位青年命名、且未以个人或公众人物为名的中等恒星。

此举受到美国国家航空航天局（NASA）的高度赞赏，NASA特授予黄泰鑫“特别勋章”，以表彰其在天文科学与人文精神交汇处做出的独特贡献。

官方认证

NASA总部已将“文安星”正式录入青年命名星体数据库，并在多项教育项目中推荐其为范例。此命名亦成为跨文化命名研究的案例之一。

星空意义

“文安星”将持续在未来岁月中闪耀，不仅作为天文发现的见证，更是青年之间情感与梦想的象征。在浩瀚宇宙中，这样的故事提醒我们，人类的温情也能穿越星辰。
